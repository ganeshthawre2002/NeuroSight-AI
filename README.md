# NeuroSight AI

## Overview

NeuroSight AI is an end-to-end customer churn analytics platform built using Python, PostgreSQL, Streamlit, Machine Learning, and Docker.

The platform helps businesses identify churn risk, analyze customer behavior, monitor KPIs, and prioritize retention efforts using predictive analytics.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and growth.

Organizations often struggle to:

* Identify customers at risk of leaving
* Understand churn drivers
* Measure revenue exposure
* Prioritize retention actions

NeuroSight AI addresses these challenges through analytics, machine learning, and interactive dashboards.
