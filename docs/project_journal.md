Analysis of 7,043 customers identified three primary churn drivers:



Month-to-month contracts exhibit a 42.71% churn rate compared to 2.83% for two-year contracts.

Customers within their first year show a 48.3% churn rate, making onboarding and early retention critical.

Churned customers pay an average monthly charge of 74.44 versus 61.27 for retained customers, suggesting pricing and perceived value may influence attrition.
